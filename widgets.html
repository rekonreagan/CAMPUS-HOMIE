Getting started with Campus homie

Use Campus homie on your computer, phone, or tablet to search, explore, and find your way around campus.On your phone, open campus homie and turn on your location.

To use place picker

  1. On your Android phone or tablet, open the Campus Homie app
  2. Click place picker
  3. Put your starting point in the 'start' section
  4. Enter your destination in the 'destination' section
  5. Show route

To use location notepad

  1. click on the location notepad
  2. Tap on the plus sign button
  3. Then enter your location
  4. Then save it

To use the alan.ai

  1. Click on the blue arrow shaped button on the home screen
  2. Speak your command
  3. Wait for your reply or action

To use the weather

  1. Click on weather feature
  2. It will display the daily climatic changes

To use chatify

  1. Click on chatify
  2. It will display a forum for free chatting
  3. You can text a message as you wait for the reply
